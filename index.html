Welcome

We are a group of elite soccer players who compete with colleges all over the northeastern United States and beyond.

The membership of PMCS consists of former Princeton Varsity soccer players, high school team captains, and casual players. Above all else, our club is a brotherhood bound by the mutual love of the beautiful game. We also like to have a good time.
